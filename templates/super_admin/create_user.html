Access Controls

👑
Super Administrator

God's Eye access. Can see all branches, manage international users, and view cross-campus analytics.

🛡️
Campus Administrator

Full control over a specific branch. Manages local users, settings, and library marks.

🎓
Head of Dept (HOD)

Department-level oversight. Can view all classes (Darajahs) within their assigned Marhala.

📋
Class Teacher

Limited to their assigned class reports and student performance tracking.

Best Practice

Use the Global scope only for regional heads or IT support. Most academic staff should be pinned to their specific campus for data integrity.
